About ICSC 2025

The International Conference on Social Computing (ICSC 2025) aims to ignite and advance collaborative research at the intersection of big data analytics and social science theory. Focused on fostering global partnerships, this conference seeks to bring together leading scholars and experts around the world, creating a unique environment for cross-cultural exchange and interdisciplinary collaboration.

A key goal of ICSC 2025 is to facilitate cutting-edge research that bridges the gap between data-driven insights and the deeper social science mechanisms that underpin human behavior. Social networks, both online and offline, play a critical role in shaping interactions, behavior patterns, and societal trends. By combining insights from social sciences with advanced analytical techniques in big data, the conference explores how social networks influence and reflect individual and collective actions.
